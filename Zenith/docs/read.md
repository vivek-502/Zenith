# About Zenith

Zenith is more than just a desktop configuration—it is a **Dynamic Desktop Orchestration Engine** for Hyprland. It is built for users who value automation, speed, and a unified aesthetic that evolves with their workflow.

### Core Features

* **Dynamic Theming:** Zenith automatically extracts colors from your current wallpaper and applies them to your entire environment, including GTK themes, Kitty terminals, Rofi menus, and Waybar.
* **Modular Styling System:** Switch between hundreds of looks instantly. Whether you want a minimalist Waybar or a complex, feature-rich Rofi menu, Zenith handles the swapping for you.
* **Smart Orchestration:** Includes advanced utilities like OCR (text recognition), an intelligent "Special Workspace" dock for window management, and persistent system tweaking (Blur, Gaps, Rounding, etc.).
* **Automated Maintenance:** Zenith’s architecture is designed to prevent "race conditions" and UI freezing by performing system updates in the background, ensuring your desktop stays snappy.
* **One-Key Customization:** Access every feature—from power profiles to icon themes—via intuitive, keyboard-driven Rofi menus.


Zenith is crafted to work *for* you, not just look good.
