
# Customizing Zenith

Zenith uses an automated system to swap configurations. Never paste your custom configs directly into the default Waybar, Rofi, or Hyprlock folders, as these will be overwritten whenever you select a new style via `SUPER + ALT + S`.

### Where to Place Your Custom Styles

To add your own themes so the system recognizes them, place them in the following directories:

#### 1. Waybar

Place your config and CSS files here:

* `~/.config/Zenith/extra-styles/waybar/YOUR_STYLE_NAME/config.jsonc`
* `~/.config/Zenith/extra-styles/waybar/YOUR_STYLE_NAME/style.css`

#### 2. Rofi Menus

Organize your menus by category. Create your subfolder under the appropriate type:

* `~/.config/Zenith/extra-styles/rofi/applauncher/YOUR_STYLE_NAME/config.rasi`
* `~/.config/Zenith/extra-styles/rofi/powermenu/YOUR_STYLE_NAME/powermenu.rasi`
* `~/.config/Zenith/extra-styles/rofi/walker/YOUR_STYLE_NAME/walker.rasi`
* `~/.config/Zenith/extra-styles/rofi/themeselector/YOUR_STYLE_NAME/theme-selector.rasi`

#### 3. Hyprlock

Place your custom lock screen config here:

* `~/.config/Zenith/extra-styles/hyprlock/YOUR_STYLE_NAME/hyprlock.conf`

### How to Apply Your Changes

1. Place your files in the directories mentioned above.
2. Press `SUPER + ALT + S` to open the **Style Switcher** menu.
3. select option from rofi, hyprlock and waybar
4. Select your `YOUR_STYLE_NAME` from the list.
